About

A filmmaker who listens before he shoots.

Based in Jakarta, Song Jae Woo is a filmmaker, photographer, and video editor with a passion for authentic visual storytelling. His work spans commercial campaigns, documentary portraits, and personal projects that blur the line between art and memory.

Every project begins with a conversation — understanding what matters, what emotion to preserve, what story only you can tell. The camera comes second.
